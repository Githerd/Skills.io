Our Quirky Story

Skill.io was born from a simple idea: learning should be fun, engaging, and a little bit unexpected.

How It All Started

In 2020, our founder, Maya, tried to learn knitting from a traditional online course. She found the experience dry, boring, and... well, not fun at all. She wondered: why can't learning new skills be as engaging as scrolling through social media or playing a game?

That's when the idea for Skill.io was born. We started with just three courses: "Knitting for Complete Beginners," "Emotional Intelligence 101," and "Everyday Makeup Magic." Today, we offer over 200 quirky skills taught by passionate experts.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at Skill.io, from course creation to community building.

Fun First

If it's not enjoyable, you won't stick with it. We design all our courses to be engaging and delightful.
